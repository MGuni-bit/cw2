My Video Portfolio

This Page features the Video content I creted throught my 3 years at De Monrfort University.

All the Videos on thiis were edited by me and all assets were eirger created by me or were sourced from copyright free websites.

If you are interested in working with me or you need a video piece. please don't hesitate contact me.

2020: Pandemonium

The title sequence (2020: Pandemonium) is based on the major events of 2020. Each scene features a different situation of 2020 (e.g COVID Outbreak). The title sequence is mostly animated and has transitions like slides and zooms. The title sequence ends with real-life footage of a protest and a fade out to the title screen.

2020:Pandemonium Coronavirus

The project is an animated cinematic title sequence and the title is 2020:Pandemonium Coronavirus with MG Productions being the production company and releasing studio. It is a spin-off of 2020:Pandemonium, a concept I created in my second academic year. This sequence is based on the impact of covid since its emergence in 2020.

E.L.E The Wrath of God

This creative piece (E.L.E. 2 The Wrath of God) is based on the monologue of Louis Farrakhan on the song “E.L.E. 2 The Wrath of God”. This piece will be mostly words with some illustrations and images. The video starts off with a black screen, then the ad libs will appear and the title of the video will be displayed. This sequence will feature a lot of transitions and animations of words and letters. The video will end with the world spinning and then cutting to black.
